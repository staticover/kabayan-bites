What is Kamayan?

Kamayan is the traditional Filipino style of eating with your hands — no utensils, just a banana leaf spread overflowing with rice, grilled meats, seafood, vegetables, and more. It's communal, joyful, and deeply rooted in Filipino culture. The perfect way to share food with the people you love.
